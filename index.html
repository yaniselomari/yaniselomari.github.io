Yanis El Omari

PhD Candidate in Finance, KU Leuven

I am a fourth-year PhD candidate in Finance at KU Leuven. My research lies in banking and financial intermediation, with a focus on systemic risk, financial market regulation, and financial stability.

My work combines finance, regulation, and computational methods to study how financial market structures and institutions shape stability and risk transmission. I also have longstanding policy experience through my work at the European Securities and Markets Authority.
